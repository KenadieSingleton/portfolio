Likes & Dislikes

I thoroughly enjoy video games, cartoons, and webcomics, as well as anime.
My favorite games are Pokemon, Story of Seasons, and Rythym games in general.
My favorite cartoons are The Powerpuff Girls and Johny Bravo,
and my favorite animes are JoJo's Bizzare Adventure and Revolutionary Girl Utena.
Lastly, my favorite webcomics are The Bittersweet Candy Bowl, Socializing 101,
Long Exposure, Love! Love! Fighting!, and Avialae, just to name a few.
These games, shows and comics have really influenced me in my art and story telling stye,
and my decision to pursue art more seriously.



There aren't many things I dislike.
